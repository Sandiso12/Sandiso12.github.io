Best VPNs for Streaming in 2025

Streaming your favorite shows from anywhere is easy with the right VPN. SmartTechLife reviews the top VPNs for 2025 to bypass geo-restrictions.

1. ExpressVPN

With 3000+ servers, ExpressVPN offers lightning-fast speeds. Priced at $12.95/month, it unblocks Netflix and Hulu.

2. NordVPN

NordVPN’s 5500+ servers and $11.95/month price make it ideal for 4K streaming and security.

3. Surfshark

At $2.49/month, Surfshark supports unlimited devices and unlocks Disney+ globally.

4. CyberGhost

With optimized streaming servers, CyberGhost costs $2.75/month and works with BBC iPlayer.

5. Private Internet Access

PIA offers 35000+ servers for $2.19/month, perfect for torrenting and streaming.
