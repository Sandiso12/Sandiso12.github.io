How to Secure Your Wi-Fi Network in 2025

With cyber threats evolving, securing your Wi-Fi is more critical than ever. SmartTechLife brings you five actionable steps to keep your network safe in 2025.

1. Change Default Passwords

Replace the default router password with a strong, unique one (e.g., 12+ characters with symbols). This prevents easy hacks.

2. Enable WPA3 Encryption

Upgrade to WPA3 for stronger encryption. Check your router settings to enable this 2025 standard.

3. Disable WPS

Wi-Fi Protected Setup can be a vulnerability. Turn it off to avoid unauthorized access.

4. Use a VPN

Add a VPN like NordVPN to encrypt your internet traffic. It’s a must for public Wi-Fi security.

5. Update Firmware Regularly

Keep your router’s firmware updated to patch security holes. Check for updates monthly.
